## Quick Start

> [!example] Get Started in 5 Steps
> 
> 1. **Mint a Genesis NFT** at [mint.bonfires.ai](https://mint.bonfires.ai/)
> 2. **Join the community** at [t.me/bonfiresai](https://t.me/bonfiresai)
> 3. **Request your Bonfire** — we'll set up your agent and connect your channels
> 4. **Let it run** — the system builds your knowledge graph automatically
> 5. **Query and explore** — use the agent and [graph.bonfires.ai](https://graph.bonfires.ai/)
> 
> The longer you run, the smarter your Bonfire gets.
