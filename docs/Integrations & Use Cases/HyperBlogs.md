You don't have to wait for [[files/Knowledge Economy/$KNOW|$KNOW]] to monetize your knowledge.

HyperBlogs are AI-generated articles, reports, and analyses created from your Bonfire's knowledge graph. They turn accumulated community knowledge into publishable, monetizable content — and they give people outside your Bonfire a way to interact with your knowledge without joining the group.

**See how it works:** [hyper.bonfires.ai](https://hyper.bonfires.ai)

## Why HyperBlogs Matter

**Content from conversations, not from scratch.** Your community already does the hard work — discussing, debating, synthesizing. HyperBlogs extract that value into a format anyone can read.

**Static anchors from a living graph.** Knowledge graphs are dynamic and always changing. HyperBlogs are snapshots — fixed-point references that capture the state of knowledge at a moment in time. Useful for reports, governance records, and audit trails.

**A window for outsiders.** Not everyone can or should join your Telegram group. HyperBlogs let external audiences — stakeholders, researchers, the public — access your community's intelligence without needing to navigate the graph directly.

**Revenue today.** Readers pay via x402 micropayments (USDC on Base). No token launch required, no future promises. Your knowledge earns now.

## Content Types

**Synthesis reports** — "What did we learn about X across all our conversations?"

**Event recaps** — "What happened at [conference]? Key themes, connections, decisions."

**Research summaries** — "State of knowledge on [topic] from our community's perspective."

**Governance records** — "What was decided, by whom, and what was the rationale?"

**Trend analysis** — "How has [topic] evolved in our discussions over time?"

**Expert roundups** — "What do our community members think about [issue]?"

## Who It's For

**Research communities** — Publish synthesis work and literature reviews. Generate revenue from intellectual work your community does organically.

**Events and conferences** — Turn conference conversations into monetized content. Attendees get access; non-attendees pay for insights.

**DAOs** — Create governance reports and decision records that external stakeholders can access. Accountability through transparency.

**Expert networks** — Your accumulated expertise becomes a product. Consulting without the scheduling overhead.

**Individual creators** — Personal Bonfires generate personal content. Your conversations, your insights, your revenue.

## Interacting with HyperBlogs

HyperBlogs aren't static PDFs. They support:

- **Voting** — Community feedback on quality and usefulness
- **Comments** — Discussion threads on published content
- **View tracking** — Analytics on readership and engagement
- **Banner generation** — AI-generated visuals for sharing

This creates a feedback loop: popular HyperBlogs signal which knowledge is valuable, informing future content generation and [[files/Knowledge Economy/Knowledge Network|Knowledge Network]] rewards.

## Getting Started

1. **Run your Bonfire** — build up knowledge over time
2. **Identify content opportunities** — what topics would people pay to learn about?
3. **Generate and publish** — create HyperBlogs from your graph via the API or DataRoom
4. **Share** — distribute links; readers pay via x402 to access
5. **Iterate** — see what resonates, generate more

The more unique and valuable your community's knowledge, the more monetization potential.
