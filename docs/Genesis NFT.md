- Compute and hosting handled by the Bonfires team

## How It Works

1. **Mint** a Genesis NFT at [mint.bonfires.ai](https://mint.bonfires.ai)
2. **Join** the community at [t.me/bonfiresai](https://t.me/bonfiresai)
3. **Request** deployment (first-come-first-served)
4. **Deposit** your NFT to activate your Bonfire
5. **Connect** your Telegram group(s)
6. **Start** building your knowledge graph

You don't have to activate immediately. Hold the NFT, sell on secondary, or redeem when ready.

## Compute Model

Each Genesis Bonfire includes a basic plan:

- **72 episodes per day** (the 20-minute processing cycle)
- **A daily allocation of agent queries**

For more compute, pay with $KNOW after TGE. The basic plan covers normal community usage — heavy research or high-volume groups can scale up.

## Why Genesis

**Less competition.** Fewer Bonfires in early stages means higher relative retrieval rates in the [[files/Knowledge Economy/Knowledge Network|Knowledge Network]]. Your [[files/Knowledge Economy/kEngrams|kEngrams]] occupy semantic space that fills up over time.

**Accumulated reputation.** Contribution history starts building now. When $KNOW emissions begin, early participants have established Bonfires already producing valuable knowledge.

**Shape the network.** Early Bonfires establish foundational knowledge. Your community's insights become reference points for future participants.

## After Year 1

The Genesis year provides hosted infrastructure at a subsidized rate. Post-TGE pricing models include:

- $KNOW credit top-ups for additional compute
- New subscription tiers
- Self-hosting remains free (open protocol)

## Can I Sell My NFT?

Yes — they're standard NFTs. Once deposited to activate a Bonfire, the NFT and its $KNOW allocation are locked to that Bonfire. Undeposited NFTs trade freely on secondary markets.
