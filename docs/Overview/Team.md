Bonfires is built by the DeSciWorld team — active in decentralised science since 2021 and focused on rebuilding connections between science and society.

## Three Prongs

**DeSciWorld** — The driving force behind Bonfires. Focused on participatory research and demonstrating how communities can leverage collective intelligence. DeSciWorld shows people how Bonfires is useful and rewarding, particularly in research contexts.

See some old docs of DeSciWorld: [gitbook](https://docs.desci.world/ecosystem/world-of-desci/scaling-desci-to-a-global-level)

**Bonfires Labs** — The solutions arm. Provides bespoke, high-quality implementations of Bonfires for clients — DAOs, research groups, conferences, network states, and teams. Revenue from Labs sustains the team while they build toward permissionless deployment.

Contact: joshua@desci.world

**Bonfires.ai** — The protocol and platform. General outreach, Genesis NFT sales, and the path toward fully onchain, permissionless deployment of Bonfires without team involvement.

## Philosophy

Build critical infrastructure that people adopt through aligned self-interest, not ideological commitment. Prove utility within local networks before broader adoption. Let network effects follow demonstrated value.
